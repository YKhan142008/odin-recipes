Lasagna Recipe

  • 2 tbsp of Olive Oil
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 800g tomato sauce
  • 300g fresh lasagna sheets
  • white sauce
  • 125g ball mozzarella

  1. To make the meat sauce, heat 2tbsp of olive oil
    in a frying pan and cook 750g lean beef mince
    in two batches for about 10 mins until browned all over.


  2. Stir through the meat mixture

  3. Pour over 800g of tomato sauce and 200ml
    hot beef stock. Then season.

  4. Bring up to the boil, then simmer for 30
    mins until the sauce is rich.

  5. Heat the oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4 and
    lightly oil an ovenproof dish (about 30 x 20cm).

  6. Spoon one third of the meat sauce into the dish,
    then cover with some fresh lasagne sheets from a 300g
    Drizzle over roughly 130g ready-made or homemade white sauce.

  7. Repeat until you have three layers of pasta.

  8. Scatter 125g mozzarella cheese over the top

  9. Bake for 45 mins
